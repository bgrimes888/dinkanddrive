Pickleball Courts in Middle Georgia

Find the best local spots to dink, drop, and drive.

🏟️ Rhythm & Rally Sports & Events

  • 📍Location: 3661 Eisenhower Parkway, Macon, GA
  • 🏢Type: Indoor facility
  • 🎾Courts: 32 courts
  • 💰Cost: ~$5 open play (reservations extra)

Rhythm & Rally is the world’s largest indoor pickleball facility, featuring 32 climate-controlled courts. Players can enjoy open play, reserve courts, or participate in leagues, clinics, and tournaments. The facility includes amenities like locker rooms, showers, and a pro shop, making it a top destination for pickleball players of all skill levels.

🌳 Tattnall Square Pickleball Center

  • 📍Location: Tattnall Square Park, Macon, GA
  • ☀️Type: Outdoor facility
  • 🎾Courts: 26 courts
  • 💰Cost: ~$2–$3 entry

The Tattnall Pickleball Center is one of the largest outdoor pickleball complexes in Georgia, featuring 26 dedicated courts. Located in downtown Macon near restaurants and shops, it offers a fun and social environment for players. The facility hosts regular games, leagues, and community events, making it a popular spot for both beginners and experienced players.

🎾 John Drew Smith Tennis Center

  • 📍Location: Macon, GA
  • ☀️Type: Outdoor (multi-use)
  • 🎾Courts: ~24 (shared with tennis)
  • 💰Cost: ~$8 daily pass

John Drew Smith Tennis Center is a large public facility that supports both tennis and pickleball play. With numerous courts available, it’s a reliable option for casual games and open play. The center offers flexible hours and is a great place for players looking for a more relaxed environment.

🏞️ South Bibb Recreation Complex

  • 📍Location: Macon, GA
  • ☀️Type: Outdoor
  • 🎾Courts: 8 courts
  • 💰Cost: ~$2 entry

South Bibb Recreation Complex provides a smaller, more casual pickleball experience with eight outdoor courts. It’s a great option for local players who want an affordable and less crowded place to play with friends or practice their skills.

🎾 Randy Stephens Tennis Center

  • 📍Location: Houston Road, Macon, GA
  • ☀️Type: Outdoor (multi-use)
  • 💰Cost: ~$2–$3 entry

Randy Stephens Tennis Center offers both tennis and pickleball courts along with lessons and events for players of all skill levels. It’s a versatile facility that hosts organized play and provides a welcoming environment for the local pickleball community.

🏞️ Jones County Pickleball Courts

  • 📍Location: Gray, GA
  • ☀️Type: Outdoor facility
  • 🎾Courts: 6–14 courts
  • 💰Cost: Free

The Jones County Pickleball Courts offer a well-maintained outdoor facility with multiple dedicated courts for players of all skill levels. With lighting, seating, and recent expansions, it provides a great space for both casual games and organized play in a growing local pickleball community.
