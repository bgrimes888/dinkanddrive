Pickleball Tournaments

🏆 Find Pickleball Tournaments

Looking to take your game to the next level? Explore official pickleball tournaments, leagues, and competitive events happening near you. Use the resources below to find upcoming tournaments, register to play, and connect with the competitive pickleball community.

Tournament Resources

USA Pickleball

The official governing body of pickleball in the United States. Their website provides information on sanctioned tournaments, rankings, rules, and events across the country.

Visit Website

PickleballTournaments.com

One of the most popular platforms for finding and registering for pickleball tournaments. You can search by location, skill level, and date to find events that match your experience.

Visit Website

Pickleheads

Pickleheads helps players find local games, courts, and tournaments. It’s a great tool for connecting with other players and discovering events in your area.

Visit Website

TeamReach

TeamReach is commonly used by pickleball groups to organize games, share schedules, and post local tournaments. Many communities use it to coordinate play and events.

Visit Website

💡 Check these platforms regularly to stay updated on new tournaments and opportunities to compete. Whether you're just starting out or looking to compete at a higher level, there’s always a place to play.
